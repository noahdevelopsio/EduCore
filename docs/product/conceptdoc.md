# Concept Document: EduCore — School Management System for Africa

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Overview

EduCore is a cloud-based School Management System (SMS) built specifically for primary and secondary schools across Africa. It digitalizes the full lifecycle of school operations — from student enrollment and attendance to fee collection and parent communication — on a single, affordable, mobile-first platform.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Most African schools — both public and private — still operate manually:
- Attendance is tracked on paper and easily lost
- Report cards are handwritten or typed in Excel
- Fee collection has no digital trail, leading to errors and fraud
- Parents have zero visibility into their child's daily school life
- School owners/proprietors have no real-time dashboard of their school's health

This leads to inefficiency, errors, mistrust, and wasted time for everyone involved.

---

## 3. Solution

EduCore provides a unified digital platform with role-based access for:
- **School Admins** — full operational control
- **Teachers** — classroom management tools
- **Students** — academic self-service portal
- **Parents** — real-time child monitoring and payments

The system works on low-bandwidth connections, is mobile-first, and supports multiple African currencies and SMS fallback for parents without smartphones.

2. Attendance Tracking (with parent SMS alerts)

## 8. Target Market

- **Geography:** Africa (starting with Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa)
- **School Type:** Private and public primary & secondary schools
- **School Size:** 50 – 2,000 students
- **Decision Maker:** School proprietor, principal, or head administrator

---

## 9. Business Model

**SaaS — Monthly Subscription per school**

| Tier | School Size | Target Monthly Price |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| Starter | < 200 students | $15 – $25/month |
| Growth | 200 – 800 students | $40 – $70/month |
| Pro | 800+ students | $80 – $150/month |

**Add-ons:**
- SMS notification bundles
- Custom branded parent app
- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Priority support

---

## 10. Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Gap EduCore Fills |
|------------|-------------------|
| Generic Western SMS tools | Not Africa-adapted; too expensive |
| Excel/paper systems | No collaboration, error-prone |
| Local basic apps | Limited features, poor UX |

EduCore's edge: **Africa-first design**, offline capability, SMS fallback, multi-currency, and an all-in-one platform at a local-market price point.

---

## 11. Africa-Specific Design Principles

- 📱 **Mobile-first** — optimized for Android low-end devices
- 🌐 **Low-bandwidth mode** — works on 2G/3G
- 📵 **Offline capability** — attendance & data sync when online
- 💬 **SMS fallback** — notifications reach all parents
- 💱 **Multi-currency** — NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR, UGX, etc.
- 🌍 **Multi-language ready** — English, French, Swahili, Hausa

## 13. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Poor internet infrastructure | Offline mode + SMS fallback |
| Low digital literacy | Simple UX + onboarding training |
| Payment collection challenges | Local payment gateways (Paystack, Flutterwave) |
| Trust & data privacy concerns | Clear data policy + local hosting options |
